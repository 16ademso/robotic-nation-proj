Who am I?

Alexa Fazio is a self-taught programmer and engineer who attends the Academy for Information Technology. Ever since she was young she had a passion for STEM. As Alexa learned more and more, she wanted to share her knowledge with others, so she started a YouTube channel called Robotic Nation in 2016. The channel contains comprehensive tutorials on a multitude of subjects such as physical computing, programming, Internet of Things (IoT), and media design.
